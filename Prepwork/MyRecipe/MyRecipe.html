Scrambled Eggs with Hotdog

My all time favorite! A quick and easy recipe perfect for breakfast, lunch or dinner, or anytime you feel hungry! Best of all it's cheap. Definitely worth it.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Pinch salt
  • Sliced bits of Hotdog(cooked separately)

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs, pinch of salt and the sliced hotdog bits in small bowl until blended
  2. Apply minimal cooking oil to a non-stick skillet
  3. Heat over medium heat for about a minute or two
  4. Pour in the egg mixture
  5. Use a spatula to push the egg mixture from one side of the skillet to the other. Repeat until eggs are cooked to desired

Warning: Serving is good for 1 person only!!! Share at your own risk!!!
